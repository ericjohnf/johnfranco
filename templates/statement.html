Statement.


"I'm not trying to change the world" anyway I don't need to, it's changing fast enough. I'm just trying to stay awake through the turmoil-- I'm following the choice and value of my attention. I'm watching the unfolding of my own thoughts, and their subsequent retreat. And yes, sometimes I allow my work to conclude, pass the time barrier and erect themselves as finished (that which only moments/days ago was the wind).

And no, finished pieces don't conclude anything. Don't contain anything, the best of them look ready for the next thing, and could be picked up and worked on immediately.

But the Possibilities need to be addressed, supported, asserted and the Joy of the Possibilities needs to be Affirmed.
